My Background

My background is in Mathematics -- specifically combinatorics and probability, specializing in the study of random graphs. I received my PhD from The Ohio State University in August 2013, under the direction of Prof. Boris Pittel.

In addition to mathematics, I have always loved programming; I have considerable experience doing so for system task automation, web development, scientific programming/simulation, and recreation. My current go-to language is Python.

My Current Role

I have accepted a position as an Insight Fellow at Insight Data Science starting June 2nd, 2014. Insight is a six-week program that takes science PhDs and prepares them for careers as Data Scientists in the technology industry.

During the program, I will be completing a data project, networking, and preparing for interviews; afterward, I will be matched up with interviews in Silicon Valley! This is a very exciting opportunity for me.
