About Me

I am a fellow at Insight Data Science. Insight is an exciting six-week program that helps scientists transition to new careers as Data Scientists in the tech industry.

My background is in Mathematics, specifically combinatorics and probability; I specialized in the study of random discrete structures and randomized algorithms. I received my PhD from The Ohio State University in August 2013, under the direction of Prof. Boris Pittel.
